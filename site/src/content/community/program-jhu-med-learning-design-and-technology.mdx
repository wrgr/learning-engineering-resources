Master's degree in Learning Design and Technology at JHU School of Education. Houses the Learning Engineering for Next-Generation Systems (LENS) concentration, which applies human systems integration and learning science to complex operational environments. The LDT program prepares graduates for careers in instructional design, learning engineering, and educational technology.
